Introduction

I am a PhD student in the Department of Astronomy and Astrophysics, TIFR, Mumbai. I conduct observational astronomy research to understand star-planet formation mechanisms. Currently, I study jets and wide-angled winds from protostars and their role in the chemical evolution of protostellar envelopes using high-resolution observations from JWST, ALMA, and uGMRT.
