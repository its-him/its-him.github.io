Introduction

I am a PhD student in the Dept. of Astronomy and Astrophysics, TIFR, Mumbai. I am doing observational astronomy to understand the formation of star-planet. Currently, I am studying jets and wide-angled winds from protostars and their role in the chemical evolution of protostellar envelopes using high-resolution observations from JWST, ALMA, and uGMRT.
